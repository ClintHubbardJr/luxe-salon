Meet the Owners

Annie Cheney and Kimberly Young opened Luxe Salon in November of 2012. They bring many years of experience and talent to their new location. Luxe Salon offers luxury hair care services by highly trained stylists. They invite you to come in and relax in their chic and cozy atmosphere.
